Buffalo Wings

back to main

Total Time: 50 mins

Description

Hot. Spicy. Crunchy. ZESTYYYY.

Hailing from Buffalo, New York, this recipe was a GENERATIONAL SECRET...until now.

These wings will KNOCK YOUR SOCKS OFF with their spicy and sour punch. Don't forget about that juicy meaty goodness encased in an irresistably crunchy coating! Legend has it that ancient merchants discovered this recipe atop the steps of Zeus's Temple. When they tried to bring it back, their ship was struck by a bolt of lightning! The ship, along with the recipe, was believed to have been lost to the great depths of the sea...never to be seen again.

Ingredients

  • 12 chicken wings
  • 1/4 teaspoon seasoned salt (or to taste)
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon course salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground paprika
  • 2 quarts vegetable oil (for frying)
  • 1 bottle (12 oz) Buffalo wing sauce

Directions

  1. Sprinkle sprinkle sprinkle!

    Season chicken wings with seasoned salt

  2. Mix mix mix!

    Mix flour, salt, black pepper, cayenne pepper, and paprika in a shallow bowl.

  3. Coat coat coat!

    Coat the wings in the flour mixture. Set in the fridge for 15 minutes.

  4. Coat coat coat! (ROUND 2)

    Coat the wings in the flour mixture (again!). Set in the fridge.

  5. Fry fry fry!

    Heat your oil in a pot until 375 F. Fry your wings until crispy and cooked through (~10-12 minutes).

  6. Sauce sauce sauce!

    Drizzle that luscious sauce all over those piping hot wings!

  7. Eat eat eat!

    Now FEAST on your glorious creation! LONG LIVE THE EMPIRE!
