Regular Fried Chicken

Total Time: 1 hr 20 mins

Description

The ORIGINAL Fried Chicken.

You can't beat the orignal! Crispy, juicy deliciousness. What more needs to be said?

Ingredients

  • 1 (4 pound) chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 2 cupts all-purpose flour (for coating)
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • salt and papper (to taste)
  • 2 quates vegetable oil (for frying)

Directions

  1. Chop chop chop!

    Take your cut up chicken pieces and skin them (if you prefer).

  2. Mix mix mix!

    In a large plastic bag, mix your flou, salt, pepper, and paprika.

  3. Dip dip dip!

    Dip your chicken pieces into the buttermilk, then place into the flour bag.

  4. Shake shake shake!

    SHAKE SHAKE SHAKE! (until coated) Then, place the coated pieces onto a tray.

  5. Wait wait wait!

    Let the pieces sit until the flour coating is paste-like. (CRUCIAL FOR CRUNCHY GOODNESS)

    While you wait, heat up a pot of oil until VERY HOT (be careful!).

  6. Fry fry fry!

    Plunk those chicken pieces into that oily bathtub until cRiSpY cRunChiTy cRuNcHy (and cooked through ~165F).

  7. Eat eat eat!

    Now FEAST on your glorious creation! LONG LIVE THE EMPIRE!
